SUPPLY CHAIN STUDENTS ASSOCIATION OF KENYA (SCSA-K)

Transforming supply chain to a professional standard.

About

SCSA-K plays a vital role in providing a platform for sharing supply chain knowledge among students, practitioners and professionals, producing highly qualified young leaders ready to spearhead change.

Motto

Transforming supply chain to a professional standard.

Mission

To promote an excellent supply chain students association in knowledge sharing across the country through effective engagements and equitable representation.

Vision

To transform and sustain an efficient supply chain students association in knowledge advancement across the country.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Accountability
  • Transparency
  • Professionalism
  • Collegiality
  • Excellence
  • Continuous Improvement

Objectives

  1. Foster development of supply chain knowledge and skills among students.
  2. Promote networking and collaboration with professionals and organizations.
  3. Provide a platform for student concerns and discussions.
  4. Organize educational and professional events.
  5. Provide mentorship opportunities.
  6. Advocate for student welfare and development.
  7. Affiliate with similar associations worldwide.

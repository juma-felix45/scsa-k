SUPPLY CHAIN STUDENTS ASSOCIATION OF KENYA (SCSA-K)

Transforming supply chain to a professional standard.

About

SCSA-K plays a vital role in providing a platform for sharing supply chain knowledge among students, practitioners and professionals, producing highly qualified young leaders ready to spearhead change.

The Supply chain students association of Kenya aims to empower and equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the field of supply chain management. It serves as a hub for education and networking providing members with access to industry insights, professional development opportunities, and a platform for collaboration among peers and experts. The association is dedicated to fostering a community of future leaders who are well-versed in the dynamics of supply chain operations and can contribute significantly to the country’s economic growth and efficiency in logistics and procurement.

Motto

Mission

To promote an excellent supply chain students association in knowledge sharing across the country through effective engagements and equitable representation.

Vision

To transform and sustain an efficient supply chain students association in knowledge advancement across the country.

Core Values

  • Integrity

    • We advocate and uphold the highest standards of honesty, ethics, and professionalism in all our actions and decisions.

  • Accountability

    • We take responsibility for our actions and their outcomes, ensuring transparency and reliability in our commitments.

  • Transparency

    • We foster an open environment where information is shared freely, promoting trust and collaboration among members.

  • Teamwork

    • We believe in the power of collaboration, valuing diverse perspectives and working together to achieve common goals.

  • Professionalism

    • We maintain a high level of competence and conduct, representing our association with dignity and respect.

  • Collegiality

    • We cultivate a supportive community where members respect and assist each other in their professional growth.

  • Excellence

    • We strive for the highest quality in our services, continuously improving and innovating to meet the evolving needs of the supply chain industry.

  • Continuous Improvement

    • We are committed to ongoing learning and development, embracing change and seeking opportunities to enhance our skills and knowledge.

Objectives

  1. Foster development of supply chain knowledge and skills among students.
  2. Promote networking and collaboration with professionals and organizations.
  3. Provide a platform for student concerns and discussions.
  4. Organize educational and professional events.
  5. Provide mentorship opportunities.
  6. Advocate for student welfare and development.
  7. Affiliate with similar associations worldwide.

Founders

Felix Abuor

"As the Founder and CEO of the Supply Chain Students Association of Kenya (SCSAK), it is an honor to champion a community of emerging supply chain leaders who are passionate about driving change in this field. I founded SCSAK in October 2023 after witnessing the challenges many young supply chain enthusiasts faced—limited access to mentorship, minimal industry exposure, and few platforms to connect with professionals. I realized that while talent and ambition existed in abundance, opportunities to nurture them were scarce. SCSAK was therefore born to bridge this gap by creating a space where young enthusiasts can gain mentorship, practical skills, and professional networks that prepare them to excel in the dynamic world of supply chain. Our mission is not only to empower individuals but also to strengthen the supply chain profession in Kenya and beyond. I extend a warm invitation to all students, professionals, and partners to collaborate with us. Together, we can nurture talent, open doors of opportunity, and reimagine the future of supply chain."
